INVESTMENT GRADE STARTUPS


Alist is an investment intelligence startup helping angel and VC investors to make better decisions about their future investments. Our core feature is ‘startup-DB’ a database of Alist-rated technology start-ups who are currently seeking funds. Each startup in our database has been rated by our algorithm based on mix of startup submitted, macroeconomic and market growth data. The top scorers are then manually reviewed and graded by our team. In other words we provide a unique list of investments with an unbiased due diligence data. All in one place.

Why Alist?


Whether you are an active angel / VC investor or a private accredited investor looking to expand your portfolio Alist is here to help. We combine unique mix of big data and human work to ensure each startup in our database meets strict, unbiased funding criteria independent of hype surrounding the tech scene, ‘buzzwords’ and opinions of tech blogs. We aim to eliminate psychological bias and human error from investment process by bringing in an independent, third-party opinion. Think of us as Moody’s for startups with investment discovery features.
